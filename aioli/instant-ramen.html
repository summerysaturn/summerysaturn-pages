Instant Ramen

Ingredients

  • Instant Ramen Packet w/ Seasoning
  • Spicy Seasoning
  • Scallions

Steps

  1. Pour 200ml of water into a pot or wok
  2. Add seasonings and bring to a boil
  3. Add noodles once water is boiling
  4. Chop and add scallions
  5. Let cook for 2-3 minutes
  6. Serve
