Tortilla Pizza Wrap

Ingredients

  • Tortillas
  • Mozz Cheeze
  • Tomato Puree
  • Onion
  • Peppers

Steps

  1. Spread tomato puree onto each tortilla
  2. Cut cheese into thin slices and cover each tortilla
  3. Chop vegetables and place on each tortilla
  4. idk bruh just stack em and roll em
  5. Oven cook for 15-20 minutes
