Dash

Double your movement at the cost of an action.

Dodge

Until the next turn, any attack roll against you has disadvantage if you can see the attacker.

Help

The target of your help action gains advantage on a check until your next turn. Alternately, you can give advantage to any ally attacking anything within 5 feet of you until your next turn.

Disengage

An action that prevents opportunity attacks for the turn.

Hide

Make a Stealth check in an attempt to hide. Success results in stealth, giving advantage on an enemy that can't see you.

Ready

Set up a trigger that results in an action after a condition is met. For example, "If the goblin steps next to me, I move away."
